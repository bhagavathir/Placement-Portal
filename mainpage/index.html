The College of Engineering, Guindy (CEG) is a public engineering college in Chennai, India and is Asia's oldest technical institution, founded in 1794. Under Tamil Nadu State Government ownership, CEG covers around 223 acres of land right in the heart of Chennai. It serves as the main campus of Anna Technological and Research University.CEG was ranked 12 among engineering colleges in India by Outlook India in 2019 and 8th by The Week.
